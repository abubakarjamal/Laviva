About Catering


Tradition since 1889

La Liva was founded in 1889 by Mr Mwangi. It was started as a small catering institution and it has been growing ever since, La Liva has had the most prominent people both Internationally and locally visit it, what makes La Liva grow is that most the leaders are usually from the family and so they are usually very committed in running the place.We only use seasonal ingredients.

La Liva seats on a famous known coast, the customers are very happy as the visit the place because they get to a view of the ocean as they are having their meals, the chef in La Liva is from Germany and he controls everything that the other chefs are doing. Indeed La Liva is the place to be.
